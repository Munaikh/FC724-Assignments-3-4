Why Do We Collect Student Data

  • Improved Instruction and Tailored Support: The data you provide helps GIC identify areas where students excel and where they might need additional support. This allows teachers to adjust their teaching methods, design specific resources, and offer personalized guidance to ensure everyone grasps the material and reaches their full potential.
  • Supports GIC's Goals: Your feedback is instrumental in helping GIC achieve its goal of providing a high-quality education for all students. By understanding students' needs and perspectives, GIC can continuously improve its curriculum, enhance its learning environment, and ensure its resources effectively support your academic journey.
  • Data Confidentiality: GIC takes student privacy seriously. All information collected through this website is anonymous and kept confidential. It will only be used for internal research and improvement purposes and will never be shared with any third-party individuals or organizations.
